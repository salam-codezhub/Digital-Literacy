Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify the sender before accepting any payment request.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
7. Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store.
8. Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions.
9. Do not respond to calls claiming urgent payment requests.
10. Immediately report fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call helpline 1930.
