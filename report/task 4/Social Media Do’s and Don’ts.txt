Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

DO’s:
1.Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts.
2.Verify information before sharing it.
3.Use privacy settings to protect your personal data.
4.Be mindful of your digital footprint.
5.Engage positively with others online.

DON’Ts:
1.Do not share personal information like phone number or address publicly.
2.Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
3.Do not spread fake news or unverified information.
4.Avoid cyberbullying or trolling.
5.Do not use weak passwords or share your account credentials.
