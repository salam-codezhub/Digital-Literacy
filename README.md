# Digital Literacy for Students

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on spreading awareness about **Digital Literacy among students**. It highlights the importance of using digital tools effectively, maintaining online safety, and building a professional online presence.

## 🎯 Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy  
- To promote safe internet practices  
- To guide students in maintaining a professional online identity  
- To introduce useful digital tools for students  

## 📖 What is Digital Literacy?
Digital literacy refers to:
- Ability to use digital tools effectively  
- Understanding online safety  
- Communicating professionally online  

## 🛠️ Useful Tools
- Google Docs & Google Drive  
- GitHub (for coding and collaboration)  
- LinkedIn (for professional profile building)  
- Online learning platforms  

## 🔐 Safe Internet Practices
- Do not share passwords  
- Avoid clicking unknown links  
- Use strong passwords  
- Enable 2-factor authentication  

## 💼 Professional Online Presence
- Keep your profile clean  
- Use real and accurate information  
- Avoid inappropriate posts  

## 🚀 Conclusion
Digital literacy is essential for students in today’s world. It helps them stay safe online, use digital tools efficiently, and build a strong professional identity.
